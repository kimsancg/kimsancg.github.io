Since my major seems slightly related to the occupation I hope to get, it still takes part in it since it is a sociology field but the admisitrative studies field will also lead me to different pathways that will provide me with bigger opportunities. Being a clinical social worker would mean that I will work with patients and their families as I provide therapy, consultation, give resources, complete plans, and create reports.

Currently I am a begining undergrad student thus my experiences in the social work field are little to none but I hope to attain some knowledge and experiences over the years in order for me to prepare for the job that I seek for. As of today I do not have a job mostly since I only study and do homework to get adjusted to the school system. But hopefully in time I get to gain some experiences that will alow me to see and understand the different work environments I could possibly be placed in the future.
